## Phase 1: Months 1-3 (Pre-Launch to First Customers)

### Goal: Find and close 3-5 paying customers

### 1.1 Direct Outreach (Manual + High-Touch)

**Timeline:** Start in Week 1, ongoing

**Strategy:** Personal emails + LinkedIn messages to target customers

**Target List Construction:**
1. Build list of 100-200 target customers:
   - HVAC companies (search: "hvac contractors near [city]" on Google Maps)
   - Real estate teams (LinkedIn search: "real estate broker," "real estate team")
   - Medical clinics (search: "[specialty] clinics near [city]")
   - Legal firms (LinkedIn: "law firm partners")

**Tools to use:**
- Clay.com or Apollo.io (find emails, phone numbers)
- Hunter.io (email finder)
- LinkedIn Sales Navigator (target by role + industry)

**Outreach Template (Email):**

```
Subject: $4K/month in lost revenue (for HVAC: "HVAC Leads You're Missing")

Hi [Name],

Quick question: How many calls do you get per month that you don't follow up on?

For [Company], we noticed [Company similar to theirs] was losing ~$4K/month in missed callbacks.

We built a simple dashboard that tracks every call and automates follow-up. Within 2 weeks, they recovered that $4K.

If you're curious to see if you have similar leaks, I'd love to show you. Takes 20 minutes.

[Calendar link to book a time]

â€” Ivo
[Your company name]
```

**Key elements:**
- Specific problem ($4K/month)
- Social proof (similar company)
- Time commitment (20 min)
- Easy CTA (calendar link)

**Expected results:**
- 2-5% response rate = 2-10 responses per 100 emails
- 30-50% of responses book a call = 1-5 demo calls per batch
- 40-60% of demos convert to trial = 1-3 trials per batch
- 25% of trials convert to paid = 0-1 paying customer per batch

**Cadence:** Send 10 emails/day (don't spam), follow up after 5 days if no response

**First Month Target:** Close 1 customer

### 1.4 Early Content Marketing (Blog + SEO)

**Timeline:** Week 3 onwards

**Goal:** Rank for long-tail keywords, capture organic traffic

**Blog Post Strategy:**

#### Post 1: "How to Track Service Business Revenue from Phone Calls (Complete Guide)"
- Target keyword: "track phone call revenue," "call tracking for service businesses"
- Format: Long-form guide (2,000 words)
- Sections:
  1. Why call tracking matters
  2. What to track (source, duration, outcome, revenue)
  3. Tools + manual methods
  4. How to attribute revenue to calls
  5. ROI calculation
  6. [CTA] "See how [Platform] does this automatically"

#### Post 2: "[Industry] Revenue Lost to Missed Calls: Here's the Data"
- Target keyword: "missed calls costing [industry]," "call response time"
- Data-driven, highly specific
- CTA: "Calculate your lost revenue" (simple calculator)

#### Post 3: "How to Automate Follow-Up for [Industry] (Without Hiring)"
- Target keyword: "automate follow-up," "SMS follow-up automation"
- Tactical, educational
- CTA: "See our automated follow-up templates"

**Publishing schedule:** 1 post/week (Weeks 3-12 = 10 posts at launch)

**SEO tactics:**
- Use Ahrefs / SEMrush to find low-competition keywords
- Interlink posts (blog to blog)
- Optimize title + meta description
- Add internal CTA to free trial in each post
- Promote on LinkedIn (drive traffic + backlinks)

**Expected results:**
- Rank in top 10 for 1-2 keywords by Month 3
- 100-500 organic visitors/month by Month 3
- 2-5 free trial signups from organic search

## Phase 2: Months 4-6 (Momentum & Scale)

### Goal: Scale to 10-15 customers, refine acquisition channels

### Activities

#### 2.1 Build Case Studies & Social Proof
- Interview 3-5 early customers
- Create 2-3 detailed case studies (write up + short video testimonial)
- Get LinkedIn recommendations from customers
- Create "customer logo" slide (show logos of 5+ customers)
- Feature case studies on website and in sales emails

**Expected impact:** 40-50% higher demo-to-paid conversion

#### 2.2 Expand Content Marketing
- Publish 12-15 more blog posts (3-4/month)
- Create 5-10 LinkedIn "carousel" posts (visual, high engagement)
- Record 3-5 short video tutorials (YouTube)
- Create lead magnet: "Revenue Recovery Checklist" (email capture)

**Expected impact:** 500-1K organic visitors/month, 5-10 trial signups/month

#### 2.3 Affiliate / Partnership Program
- Reach out to agencies, consultants, Zoho partners
- Offer 20-30% commission for customer referrals
- Create "partner onboarding kit" (one-pager, email template, demo access)

**Target partners:**
- Digital marketing agencies
- Business consultants
- CRM / software resellers
- Industry associations (HVAC contractors, real estate boards)

**Expected impact:** 1-3 customers/month from partners

#### 2.4 Webinar / Virtual Event
- Host "How to Track Revenue for [Industry]" webinar
- Guest speaker (customer or industry expert)
- Promote on LinkedIn, email list, content
- Record and repurpose as YouTube content

**Expected impact:** 20-50 attendees, 2-5 trial signups

#### 2.5 Paid Advertising Expansion
- Scale Google Ads budget to $500-1K/month (if ROAS >3:1)
- Expand LinkedIn to lookalike audiences
- Start testing Facebook/Instagram ads (lower cost, younger audience)
- Retargeting ads (website visitors who don't convert)

**Expected impact:** 10-20 trials/month from paid channels

#### 2.6 Sales Automation (If You Get Help)
- Hire or contract 1 sales development rep (SDR) for outbound
- Run 50-100 cold outreach campaigns/month
- Build email sequence for outbound leads
- Implement Outreach.io or Salesloft for tracking

**Expected impact:** 2-5 customers/month from outbound

## Phase 3: Months 7-12 (Scale & Optimization)

### Goal: Scale to 50+ customers, refine acquisition, focus on retention

### Activities

#### 3.1 Sales Team Expansion
- Hire 1 full-time "Sales Closer" (handles demos and closes)
- You focus on strategy, customer research, positioning
- Closer focuses on: demos, proposals, negotiation, contract

**Expected impact:** 2-3x more closes (you can't be bottleneck)

#### 3.2 Double Down on What Works
- Identify top 2-3 acquisition channels (probably: direct outreach, content, partners)
- Allocate 70% of effort to those
- Drop/minimize low-performing channels

**Example:** If direct outreach + partners are working:
- Hire outbound SDR (scale cold outreach)
- Expand partner program (recruit 5-10 more partners)
- Reduce paid ad spend if not profitable

#### 3.3 Advanced Content + SEO
- Publish 50+ blog posts total (rank for 20+ keywords)
- Create 10-15 video tutorials (YouTube channel)
- Launch email newsletter (weekly tips for subscribers)
- Create industry-specific resource centers (HVAC, Real Estate, Medical)

**Expected impact:** 1K-2K organic visitors/month, 10-20 organic trial signups/month

#### 3.4 Product Marketing
- Create detailed feature demos (YouTube playlist)
- Develop industry-specific workflows (HVAC workflow, RE workflow, Medical workflow)
- Case study library (10+ public case studies on website)
- Comparison page ("vs competitors" or "is this right for you?")

**Expected impact:** Faster sales cycles, higher conversion rates

#### 3.5 Customer Success & Retention
- Hire 1 customer success manager (onboarding, retention, upsell)
- Build onboarding program (reduce churn, faster time-to-value)
- Monthly check-in calls (ensure success, find upsell opportunities)
- Community building (monthly Slack/Zoom calls for customers)

**Expected impact:** 80%+ retention, 40%+ upsell to higher tier

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) & Lifetime Value (LTV)

### Assumptions
- Average contract value (ACV): $24K/year ($2K/month)
- Average customer lifespan: 24 months (2 years)
- LTV: $48K (assuming no churn; conservative estimate)

### CAC by Channel

| Channel | Cost/Customer | Scale Potential | Timeline |
|---------|---|---|---|
| Direct Outreach | $500-1K | 5-10/month | Months 1-6 |
| LinkedIn Content | $0 (time only) | 1-3/month | Months 2-12 |
| Blog / Organic | $0 (time only) | 1-5/month | Months 3-12 |
| Paid Ads (Google) | $2K-3K | 5-20/month | Months 4-12 |
| Partners/Affiliates | $400-600 (commission) | 2-5/month | Months 4-12 |
| Sales Outbound | $800-1.2K | 10-30/month | Months 6-12 |

### Blended CAC Target
- Month 1-3: $1-2K/customer (high because volume low, you doing everything)
- Month 4-6: $800-1.2K/customer (channels proven, some scaling)
- Month 7-12: $500-800/customer (sales team, optimization)

### LTV:CAC Ratio
- Target: >3:1 (sustainable)
- Your target: $48K LTV, $500-1K CAC = 48:1 (excellent)

## Sales Process

### Stage 1: Lead Generation (Awareness)
**Goal:** Get them to demo request

**Tactics:**
- Outreach email / LinkedIn DM
- Blog content (calls for demos)
- Free trial signup
- Ad click â†’ landing page

**Conversion:** 2-5% of reaches â†’ demo request

### Stage 2: Demo / Discovery Call (Consideration)
**Goal:** Understand their pain, show fit

**Format:** 30-min call
- Your story (5 min): "Here's what we do, who it helps"
- Their story (10 min): "Tell me about your business, challenges"
- Live demo (10 min): Show relevant features (Tier 1 or 2, based on them)
- Next steps (5 min): "Here's pricing, here's the free trial, let's try it"

**Conversion:** 40-60% of demos â†’ trial signup

### Stage 3: Free Trial (Decision)
**Goal:** Get them to see ROI, decide to pay

**Duration:** 14 days

**Trial experience:**
- Day 0: Welcome email, sample calls in dashboard
- Day 2: "Here's what we found" (summary of their calls)
- Day 5: "You're saving time. Here's what you're missing with paid"
- Day 10: "One week left. Ready to keep going?"
- Day 14: "Your trial ends tomorrow. 20% off if you keep it"

**Conversion:** 20-30% of trials â†’ paid

### Stage 4: Onboarding (Post-Sale)
**Goal:** Customer success, retention, upsell

**Process:**
- Day 0: Onboarding call (you set up)
- Day 1-3: Check-ins (ensure they're using platform)
- Week 2: Follow-up (first insights, quick wins)
- Month 1: Business review (ROI summary, optimization ideas)
- Month 3: Upsell check (ready to move from Tier 1 â†’ 2, or 2 â†’ 3?)

**Conversion:** 80%+ retention, 30-40% upsell
